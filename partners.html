Strategic Alliances

Built on trust. Strengthened by performance.

Our partner network is one of our most valuable assets. By forging long-term relationships with industry-leading manufacturers, service providers and financial institutions, Aristopole Group is able to offer our clients access to the world's best products and services — backed by local knowledge and regional reach.

Zhongxin Heavy Industry

One of China's leading manufacturers of crushing and screening equipment, Zhongxin Heavy Industry produces the XHP Series hydraulic cone crushers, jaw crushers, impact crushers and complete stone production plants. Aristopole Group is the authorised representative for Zhongxin products in the DRC, providing full supply, commissioning and after-sales support.

Shiptech

Aristopole Group's petroleum supply partner, Shiptech provides access to a wide range of petroleum products including diesel, heavy fuel oils, industrial lubricants and specialty fluids. Through this partnership, we are able to supply bulk fuel and petroleum products reliably to remote mining and industrial sites across the DRC.

Pamwe Mining Services

A specialist mining services company, Pamwe Mining Services partners with Aristopole Group to deliver comprehensive aftermarket services to mining operations in the DRC. Their technical teams provide planned maintenance, equipment overhauls, field repairs and component rebuilds across a range of mobile and fixed plant equipment.

ACTOM

ACTOM is Africa's largest manufacturer, repairer and distributor of electro-mechanical equipment. Through our partnership with ACTOM, Aristopole Group supplies a comprehensive range of electrical distribution equipment, motors, transformers and switchgear to mining and industrial clients in the DRC.

GNK Valves is a leading supplier of industrial valves and pipeline products, including gate valves, ball valves, butterfly valves and check valves for process, water, mining and industrial applications.
