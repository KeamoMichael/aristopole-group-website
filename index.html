Who We Are

About Aristopole Group

Aristopole Group DRC SARL is Africa's leading trading, procurement and supply company, delivering world-class supply chain management to clients across the mining, energy, agriculture and construction sectors.

Registered in the DRC with offices in Lubumbashi and Kinshasa, our international management team brings extensive market experience and deep regional networks.

"To provide essential value to your supply chain in order to advance and overcome the obstacles of doing regional trade." — Aristopole Group Mission Statement
