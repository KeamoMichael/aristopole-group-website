Why Aristopole

A place where your work shapes a continent.

At Aristopole Group, you are not just filling a role — you are part of an organisation at the forefront of Africa's industrial and economic development. The work we do connects global supply networks to the DRC's most critical industries, and the people who do that work are our greatest asset.

We are an international team, built on a culture of high performance, continuous learning and mutual respect. We value initiative, reward excellence and create real opportunities for growth — in Africa and beyond.

Opportunities

Where you could fit in.

We hire across three broad functional areas. Whether your background is in logistics and operations, engineering and technical services, or commercial and business development — there may be a place for you at Aristopole Group.

Operations & Logistics

Our operations and logistics team is the backbone of Aristopole Group — managing procurement, freight, warehousing, customs clearance and last-mile delivery across the DRC. We look for organised, resilient professionals with experience in supply chain management, freight forwarding or warehouse operations.

  • Supply Chain Coordinator — Kinshasa
  • Logistics Manager — Lubumbashi
  • Warehouse Supervisor — Kinshasa / Lubumbashi
  • Customs & Compliance Officer — Kinshasa
  • Procurement Specialist — Kinshasa
Apply for this area

Engineering & Technical

Our engineering and technical team supports clients with product selection, equipment commissioning, maintenance planning and field service. We work with hydraulic systems, pumps, crushing equipment and mining machinery, and we value engineers who combine technical depth with practical problem-solving skills.

  • Mechanical Engineer — Mining Equipment (Lubumbashi)
  • Hydraulic Systems Technician — Kinshasa / Lubumbashi
  • Field Service Engineer — Mining (DRC-wide)
  • Crushing Plant Specialist — (Zhongxin equipment)
  • Electrical & Instrumentation Technician
Apply for this area

Business Development

Our commercial team drives new client acquisition, manages key accounts and develops our strategic partnerships across the DRC and beyond. We look for commercially astute, entrepreneurial individuals with a genuine passion for Africa's business landscape and strong networks in the mining, construction or industrial sectors.

  • Business Development Manager — Mining Sector (Lubumbashi)
  • Key Account Manager — Industrial Supplies (Kinshasa)
  • Commercial Analyst — Kinshasa
  • Marketing & Communications Coordinator
  • Partnership Development Executive — Africa

Our Culture

What we look for in every hire.

Integrity

Drive
