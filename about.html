Who We Are

A strategic supply partner built for Africa.

Aristopole Group DRC SARL is a leading trading, procurement and supply company headquartered in Kinshasa, Democratic Republic of Congo. We specialise in fueling sustainability across Africa by delivering world-class supply chain management capabilities to clients across the mining, energy, agriculture and construction sectors.

Founded with a clear mandate — to bridge the gap between global suppliers and African operators — Aristopole Group has grown into a trusted name across the DRC, underpinned by an international management team with deep regional expertise and a resilient balance sheet that ensures long-term reliability.

With offices in both Kinshasa and Lubumbashi, we are strategically positioned at the heart of central African commerce, enabling us to serve clients across the country's most economically active provinces with speed and precision.

Our Vision
