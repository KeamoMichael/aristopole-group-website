Product Portfolio

Comprehensive supply across six product categories.

Aristopole Group maintains an extensive catalogue of products spanning mining, industrial, agricultural and construction applications. Our global sourcing network and established supplier relationships ensure quality products are available with competitive lead times and pricing.

01

Hydraulic & Pumping Systems

From high-pressure hydraulic components to industrial pump systems, we supply a comprehensive range of fluid power solutions for mining, construction and industrial applications. Our product range covers everything from complete pump stations to individual fittings and seals.

Centrifugal and submersible pumps (Grundfos)
Hydraulic power units and cylinders (HYDAC)
High-pressure hoses, fittings and couplings
Solopump dewatering and slurry pumps
Hydraulic filtration and fluid management systems
Pressure gauges, valves and instrumentation
GrundfosHYDACSolopump
02

Mining Chemicals

The DRC's mineral processing operations require a reliable supply of specialised mining chemicals for flotation, leaching, solvent extraction and electrowinning processes. Aristopole Group sources and supplies a broad range of mining reagents and process chemicals.

Flotation reagents — collectors, frothers and depressants
Leach chemicals — sulphuric acid, lime and cyanide
Solvent extraction reagents (SX/EW circuit chemicals)
Scale and corrosion inhibitors
Grinding media and blasting accessories
Water treatment chemicals and flocculants
03

Agricultural Equipment

Africa's agricultural potential is vast. Aristopole Group supplies modern agricultural machinery and equipment to support farming operations across the DRC — from smallholder irrigation to large-scale commercial agriculture — contributing directly to food security and rural economic development.

Irrigation systems — drip, pivot and sprinkler systems
Agricultural pumps and water management equipment
Tractors and mechanised tillage equipment
Grain storage silos and post-harvest handling
Fertiliser spreaders and crop protection equipment
Solar-powered irrigation and off-grid solutions
04

Construction Machinery

Infrastructure development across the DRC demands access to quality construction machinery and equipment. Aristopole Group supplies a wide range of construction plant and machinery, from earthmoving equipment to concrete production and stone crushing systems for road and civil construction.

Excavators, bulldozers and motor graders
Crushing and screening plants for road aggregate
Concrete batching plants and mixers
Articulated dump trucks and rigid haulers
Compaction equipment — rollers and plate compactors
Scaffolding, formwork and temporary structures
Zhongxin Heavy Industry
05

Steel & Industrial Materials

Through partnerships with NJR Steel, Macsteel Exports and ACTOM, Aristopole Group supplies a comprehensive range of steel products and industrial materials to construction, mining and manufacturing operations across the DRC. From structural steel to precision engineered components.

Structural steel — beams, columns, angles and channels
Steel plates, sheets, coils and strips
Pipes, tubes and hollow sections
Industrial valves and pipeline fittings (GNK Valves)
Fasteners — HUCK and TLP bolting systems
Electrical distribution equipment (ACTOM)
NJR SteelMacsteelACTOMGNK ValvesHUCKTLP
06

Underground Trackless Equipment

Underground mining operations in the DRC demand specialised trackless mobile machinery designed for confined, challenging environments. Aristopole Group supplies and supports a full range of underground trackless equipment built to perform in Africa's most demanding mine environments.

Load-haul-dump (LHD) machines and scooptrams
Underground drill rigs — face drilling and production drilling
Underground haul trucks and personnel carriers
Bolting and scaling machines for ground support
Shotcrete sprayers and ground reinforcement
Spare parts and rebuild kits for all major OEM brands
